链接

题意

Let $f_n(x)=f(f_{n−1}(x))$ and $f_1(x)=f(x)$, he define the power of a number $x$ as:

$$g(x)=\lim_{n\rightarrow+\infty}\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^nf_i(x)$$

He wants to know whether $x$ has the same power for all $x\in[1,n]$.
